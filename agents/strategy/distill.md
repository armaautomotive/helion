# Strategy Distillation Instructions

When reviewing company-wide documents, extract information useful for strategic planning and decision-making.

Prioritize:
- product strengths and weaknesses
- repeat customer pain points
- signs of strongest market fit
- pricing clues and budget sensitivity
- competitive signals
- sales patterns and objections
- operational constraints that affect growth
- strategic opportunities by market, region, or customer type

Ignore or de-prioritize:
- routine admin details without strategic impact
- repetitive operational notes unless they reveal a broader pattern
- low-signal internal noise

Preferred distilled files:
- market_opportunities.md
- strategic_risks.md
- positioning_notes.md
- pricing_signals.md
- execution_constraints.md
