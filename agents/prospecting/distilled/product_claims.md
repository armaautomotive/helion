# Product Claims

## Safe core positioning
- Portable CNC tube notching machine
- Intended for shops needing repeatable tube coping/notching work
- Relevant where fit-up quality, throughput, or setup time affects profitability
- Designed to provide automated tube fabrication capability without requiring a large permanent machine installation

## Credible capability statements
- Uses a plasma torch
- Requires a customer-supplied plasma torch with remote trigger
- Requires compressed air
- Supports round steel tube and pipe
- Supports round aluminum tube and pipe
- Supports approximately 1 inch to 4 inches outer diameter
- Can cut straight lengths
- Can cut coped intersections at angles
- Can support more than two-tube intersection joints
- Can cut tabs and corresponding slots for fit-up
- Can cut connected pie-cut sections
- Can engrave part labels, directions, bend locations, and bend orientations

## Important limits to keep explicit
- Not intended for solid rod
- Not intended for square or rectangular tube
- Not intended for angle/L-section or other non-round profiles
- Plasma is less accurate than laser
- Machine is a 2.5-axis system, not a 4-axis articulated cutting machine

## Commercial facts safe to repeat
- Standard price direction: $12,000 USD
- Launch price direction: $10,500 USD for first 5 units
