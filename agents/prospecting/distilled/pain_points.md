# Pain Points

## Operational problems the CNC tube notcher can address
- Slow manual tube notching workflows
- Inconsistent notch quality between operators
- Rework from poor tube fit-up
- Fabrication bottlenecks around cutting/coping
- Difficulty scaling repeatable tube fabrication jobs
- Excess setup time on recurring parts
- Labor cost tied to skilled manual fitting work

## Why these problems matter
- Poor fit-up slows welding and assembly
- Inconsistency increases scrap or rework risk
- Manual methods make lead times harder to control
- Repetitive cutting work consumes skilled shop capacity
- Growth in production volume exposes process inefficiency

## Pain points especially relevant to good prospects
- Shops producing roll cages, frames, chassis parts, or welded round-tube assemblies
- Teams with multiple operators where consistency matters
- Shops trying to move from custom-only work toward more repeatable production
