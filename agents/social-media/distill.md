# Distillation Instructions: Social Media

Purpose:
- Convert broad company material into a compact knowledge base for spotting and handling relevant public discussions.

What to extract:
- Product problems that map well to community questions or shop frustrations
- Safe product claims and concise explanations suitable for public replies
- Good engagement patterns for answering questions without sounding promotional
- Disqualifiers for when not to engage
- Audience segments and communities where the product is likely to be relevant

What to ignore unless clearly relevant:
- Internal detail that does not help identify or answer public opportunities
- Legal/admin material with no impact on community engagement
- Long background narratives that can be reduced to a few reusable reply principles

Output expectations:
- Write concise markdown files into `distilled/`
- Prefer reusable bullet lists, response angles, and fit/disqualifier rules
- Keep a clear line between factual support language and sales-oriented messaging

Recommended distilled files:
- `community_fit_signals.md`
- `engagement_rules.md`
- `safe_product_claims.md`
- `reply_patterns.md`
- `disqualifiers.md`

Quality bar:
- Another session should be able to review a public thread and decide quickly whether and how to engage
- The distilled context should stay compact enough for routine loading and manual review
